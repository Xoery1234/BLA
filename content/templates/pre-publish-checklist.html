Honest Assessment

This checklist is team discipline. da.live doesn't enforce it technically — but we do as a team. Publishing is immediate and irreversible: there is no staging environment between preview and live. Brand and legal errors on a live page create real commercial and reputational risk.

A completed checklist is your evidence that content, brand, and legal sign-off happened before publishing. We agreed to this standard as part of our launch process. This document is how we hold each other accountable. Automated approval workflow is planned for a future release — until then, this checklist is the gate.

Every first publish during Stage 1 must have a completed checklist on file. No exceptions.
